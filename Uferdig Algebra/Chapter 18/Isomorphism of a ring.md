> An isomorphism $\phi : R \to R'$ from a [[Ring|ring]] $R$ to a ring $R'$ is a [[Homomorphism of a ring|homomorphism]] that is one to one and onto $R$. The rings $R$ and $R'$ are then isomorphic.
