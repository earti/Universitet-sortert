
> [!abstract] %%Oppsummering%%
> For rings $R$ and $R'$, a map $\phi : R \to R$ is a homomorphism if the following two conditions are satisfied for all $a,b \in R$: 
> 1. $\phi(a +b) =\phi(a)+\phi(b)$, 
> 2. $\phi(ab) = \phi(a)\phi(b)$.
