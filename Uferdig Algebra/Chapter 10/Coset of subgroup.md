Let $H$ be a [[Subgroup|subgroup]] of a [[Group|group]] $G$. The [[Delmengde|subset]] $aH =\{ah|h \in H\}$ of $G$ is the left coset of $H$ containing $a$, while the subset $Ha =\{ha|h \in H\}$ is the right coset of $H$ containing $a$.
