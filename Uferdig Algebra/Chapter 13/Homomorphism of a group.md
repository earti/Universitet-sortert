
> [!abstract] %%Oppsummering%%
> A map $\phi$ of a [[Group|group]] $G$ into a group $G'$ is a homomorphism if the homomorphism property $$\phi(ab) = \phi(a)\phi(b)$$ holds for all $a,b \in G$.
