
> [!abstract] %%Oppsummering%%
> Let $\phi : G \to G'$ be a [[Homomorphism|homomorphism]] of [[Group|groups]]. The [[Subgroup|subgroup]] $\phi^{−1}[\{e\}] = \{x \in G|\phi(x) = e\}$ is the kernel of $\phi$, denoted by $\ker(\phi)$.
